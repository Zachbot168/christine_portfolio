Christine Shin

Storyteller, strategist, and creative visionary bridging cultures through multimedia narratives and impactful campaigns.

A Global Perspective, Local Impact

Christine Shin is a Northwestern University journalism student whose work spans continents and causes. From managing partnerships with 100+ global influencers at Seoul-based fashion brand Lewkin to directing documentaries that raised $13,000 for underprivileged communities in the Philippines, she brings both strategic thinking and humanitarian heart to every project.

Her multimedia expertise shines through roles at Northwestern Admissions, where she creates authentic student stories, and at Philstar National Newspaper, where her content reached 15 million users. As Publisher of nuAZN Magazine and Head of Ad Sales at North by Northwestern, she's proven her ability to build brands, secure partnerships, and drive meaningful engagement.

With an International Baccalaureate background from Manila and current studies in Journalism and International Studies with an Integrated Marketing Communications Certificate, Christine bridges cultures through compelling visual narratives—whether capturing the spirit of adventure, the texture of everyday moments, or the serenity found in quiet contemplation.
